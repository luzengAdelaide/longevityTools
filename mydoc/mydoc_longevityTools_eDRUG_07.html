    Connectivity maps enrichment analysis

    The connectivity maps approach is a rank-based enrichment method utilizing the KS test (Lamb et al., 2006). It measures the similarities of expression signatures based on the enrichment of up- and down-regulated genes at the top and bottom of sorted (ranked) gene lists.
